Appraisal Cycle Process

User can track the workflow of the Performance Management application.

Step 1: Appraisees will receive a reminder notification one day prior to the start of the appraisal cycle, informing them about the upcoming review.

Once the appraisal cycle begins, appraisees will see their assigned Key Responsibility Areas (KRAs) categorized by template in the "Self Review" section on the homepage. They can review the KRAs in detail, sign off on their self-assessment, and submit it for approval.



When the user fills out their self-review, the attachment icon is also available.


By clicking on the icon, the user can add an attachment and then click the Save button to upload it.


After that, the user will see an eye icon in the same column. By clicking on this icon, they can view their attachment.


After the user submits their self-review, their manager will see the review under 'My Teams.' When the manager reviews it, the same column will display the eye icon, allowing the manager to view the attachment.


And then, by clicking on the eye icon, the manager can check the user's attachment and also download it if needed.


Appraisees will see the following screen under the "Self Review" section when the self-review period has expired. They can request an extension by clicking on the "Click Here" link.


The HR team receives the extended appraisal days request notification and sees the request on home page under the requests tab. To extend they can add a day number to be extended in the extension box and click on extend.


If a reason is not provided, HR has the authority to reject the request during the validation process.


Step 2 (Appraiser Review or Manager Review)

Appraisers will be notified about the appraiser review cycle of their appraisee, with a notification one day before the self-review timeline ends (appraiser review initiation) and a reminder on the day of the self-review deadline (appraiser review reminder).

Under the "My Teams" section, managers or appraisers can find their appraisees and the status of their employee's self-review (Not Started, Pending, or Completed).

Once the appraisee completes their self-review, the "Employee Review Status" column will update to "Completed." An action or edit icon will appear in the "Action" column, allowing the appraiser to begin the review process by clicking on it.


By clicking on the edit icon, the manager review page will open, where the manager can review their appraisee. The appraiser or manager can select ratings from the Appraiser’s rating dropdown and add comments for each KRA category.


Once the appraiser completes the ratings and comments, they can sign and submit the review by clicking the "Sign" button.


The appraiser can view both the appraisee's self-review rating and their own rating (the overall rating) on the manager review page under the "Review" section.


Additional Reviews: In this section, the appraiser can request additional reviews for their appraisee from the appraisee's previous manager.

To send a request, click the “Additional Review” option on the manager review page. The "Add Additional Reviewer" button will appear, where the appraiser can select the previous manager, input weightage, and click "Save."

Name: The appraiser can enter the name of the appraisee’s previous manager from whom an additional review could be requested or taken.

Weightage: The appraiser can specify the percentage weightage for the rating provided by the previous manager. If the appraiser assigns a percentage to an additional reviewer, that percentage will be deducted from the appraiser’s initial rating.

Initially, the appraiser’s rating carries a weightage of 100%. If an additional reviewer is assigned a percentage, the average of the appraiser’s rating and the additional reviewer’s rating will be used to calculate the final rating, which will be considered the appraiser’s review.



The Manager can find additional review requests under “Additional Reviews”. Click on the edit icon of the action column displays the “Additional Review” page.


On the “Additional review” page, The Manager can review by selecting the Appraisers rating dropdown and adding remarks accordingly for each category KRAs and finally submit their review by clicking on submit button.



Step3: (HR Review)

HR roles users (HR Managers, HR Heads, and HR Admins) will be notified about the HR review of the appraisee during the appraisal process. HR can receive notifications about the HR review one day before the appraiser review timeline, called the HR review announcement, and again on the day of the appraiser review timeline, called the appraisal HR reminder notification.

HR Executives
HR Executives can check the review statuses and completed reviews of appraisees and appraisers by clicking on the edit icon in the "Action" column.

HR Executives can extend the self-review period upon appraisee request, view both appraisee and appraiser reviews, but cannot edit the appraiser’s review.


HR can view the ratings and comments of both appraisees and appraisers on the “HR Review” page by clicking the edit or pencil icon in the "Action" column.


HR Manager, HR Heads, and HR Admins

HR Managers, HR Heads, and HR Admins can review and check the ratings and comments of both appraisees and appraisers. They have the ability to edit the appraiser’s and additional reviewer’s ratings and comments if necessary. By clicking on the edit icon in the "Action" column, they can access the "HR Review" page.



Clicking the edit icon for the “Appraiser’s Rating” or “Appraiser’s Rating Remarks” will display the "Edit Appraiser Review" page, where ratings and comments can be edited.


Similarly, clicking the edit icon for the additional manager’s rating or remarks column will open the "Edit Additional Review" page, where ratings and comments can be edited.

Under the "Overall Rating" section of the HR Review page, the Self-rating, Appraiser rating, and the final rating (calculated as the average of the Appraiser and Additional Reviewer ratings) are displayed.

The final rating column will only be displayed if an additional reviewer has provided feedback on the appraisee.


HR Head, HR Head admin

HR Head and HR Head admin can check the reviews status of appraisees, only the appraisee self-review and their appraiser review are completed they can finally release the appraisal conformation.


Go to the release option on the “Home” page, type the comments, and submit them. All appraisers will be notified about the release. The appraisal cycle process completes at this point.
