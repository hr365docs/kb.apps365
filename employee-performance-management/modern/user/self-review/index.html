    Self Review

    In self-review, the user can give himself a rating according to his performance and can review his rating.

    1. Review
    2. Summary and Plan

    Review

    Within the review section, various categories—such as Skills, Product, and Sales—are configured by the administrator. Each category includes specific Key Responsibility Areas (KRAs), under which targeted questions are defined for the user.




    Users are required to evaluate their performance by providing self-ratings for each question. They may also include remarks to justify or elaborate on their ratings. This self-assessment contributes to the overall performance evaluation.




    In the Overall Rating section, users can view their consolidated self-rating presented in three formats: Simple Rating, Weighted Rating, and Absolute Rating, offering a comprehensive view of their performance metrics.


    Summary and Plan

    The Summary and Plan section serves as a reflective feedback module where users provide insights into their performance and outline future objectives.




    1. Achievements: In this section, users describe key accomplishments from the past year, highlighting significant contributions and milestones. After completing this section, click Save and Next to proceed to the next tab.



    2. Self Reflections: Users reflect on their strengths, competencies, and overall performance during the review cycle. This includes self-assessment ratings, goal achievements, and personal insights.



    3. Employee's Action Plan: Here, users outline their strategic plans and objectives for the upcoming period, detailing how they intend to achieve future goals. After completing this section, click Save and Next to move forward.


    4. Summary by Employee:  In this section, the user provides a comprehensive summary of their overall performance. This includes a reflection on past achievements, contributions made during the year, and future goals or plans for continued growth and success.
